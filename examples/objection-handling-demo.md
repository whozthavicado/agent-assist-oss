# Objection Handling Demo

## Input

Customer: "It's too expensive. I'll think about it."

## Detected signals

- price objection
- hesitation
- low commitment

## Suggested reply

"I understand. Many customers compare options first. Would it help if I show you the version that fits your budget better, or explain what makes this option worth the price?"

## CRM note

Customer raised a price objection and did not commit during the conversation. Interest remains possible, but the customer wants more time before deciding.

## Summary

The customer showed interest but hesitated because of price. Recommended next step: clarify value, offer a budget-fit option if available, and schedule a follow-up.
